CAD to BIM Services Built for Accuracy and Workflow Fit

Eagle BIM delivers CAD to BIM services that transform your legacy 2D drawings into intelligent, coordinated 3D Revit models — without the manual rework that slows firms down. Whether you are working from DWG files, scanned PDFs, paper drawings, or a mix of all three, our CAD to BIM conversion process produces clean, LOD-compliant Revit models that plug directly into your existing workflow.

Our CAD to BIM conversion covers all disciplines — architectural, structural, MEP, and civil. Every model is built to your naming conventions, view templates, title block standards, and BIM Execution Plan requirements. We do not deliver generic models that need hours of cleanup. We deliver production-ready Revit files your team can use from the moment they open them.

Firms use our CAD to BIM services to unlock legacy drawing libraries, prepare existing buildings for renovation, migrate from 2D CAD to full BIM, and meet owner BIM requirements on projects where the design originated in AutoCAD. Whatever your reason for converting, Eagle BIM gets it done accurately and on time.

What We Convert

Core CAD to BIM Conversion Services

Every CAD to BIM services engagement is handled by experienced Revit modelers who understand documentation standards across all disciplines and project types.

Architectural CAD to BIM Conversion

2D architectural DWG and PDF drawings converted into complete Revit architectural models. Walls, floors, roofs, ceilings, doors, windows, and stairs built to your title block, view template, and naming convention standards. Output supports SD, DD, and CD phase documentation.

Structural CAD to BIM Conversion

Structural 2D drawings — framing plans, sections, and foundation layouts — converted into coordinated Revit structural models. Steel, concrete, and precast elements modeled at the required LOD with connection details and slab openings included.

MEP CAD to BIM Conversion

Mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and fire protection 2D drawings converted into coordinated 3D MEP Revit models. Duct routing, pipe runs, conduit, and equipment placement modeled from CAD source data and ready for coordination and clash detection.

2D to BIM Conversion for Renovation Projects

Existing building drawings — often incomplete, outdated, or held only as PDFs — converted into accurate 3D BIM models for use as the base model on renovation, extension, and retrofit projects. Field verification recommendations provided where source data accuracy is uncertain.

DWG to Revit Conversion

Direct DWG to Revit conversion for architectural, structural, and MEP disciplines. AutoCAD layer structure mapped to Revit categories and system families. Parametric intelligence, schedules, and annotation standards applied to match your BIM template precisely.

PDF to BIM Conversion

Scanned and vector PDF drawings converted into intelligent 3D Revit models. Dimensions verified during conversion, and any discrepancies or unclear details in the source PDF are flagged and resolved with your team before the model is finalized.

LOD Compliance

LOD Output for Every Project Stage

LOD level is agreed in your BIM Execution Plan before conversion begins — and we deliver to it without deviation.

LOD 100
Conceptual
Overall massing and approximate size. Suitable for feasibility and early design review stages.
LOD 200
Schematic
Approximate geometry with basic properties. Suitable for schematic design and area calculations.
LOD 300
Defined
Accurate geometry and relationships. Standard output for design development and coordination.
LOD 400
Fabrication
Full fabrication-level detail with connections, fixings, and assembly information for construction.
