BIM Consulting Services Built for How AEC Firms Actually Work

Eagle BIM delivers BIM consulting services that help architecture, engineering, and construction firms adopt BIM effectively, standardize their practices, and get measurable value from their technology investment. As a dedicated BIM consulting company, we do not sell generic advice — we assess your current workflows, identify exactly what is slowing your team down, and build practical solutions that stick.

Whether your firm is making the move from 2D CAD to full BIM for the first time, scaling BIM across multiple offices, or trying to cut coordination failures on complex projects, our BIM consultants bring the hands-on experience to guide you through every stage. We have worked across commercial, healthcare, industrial, residential, and institutional projects — so we understand the pressures your team faces.

Every engagement is practical, not theoretical. We deliver BIM Execution Plans your team will actually follow, Revit templates your modelers will actually use, and workflows that reduce the manual effort that slows projects down. The goal is always the same: better models, fewer errors, and faster delivery on every project.

Firms Transitioning from CAD to BIM
You have been working in 2D and need to move to Revit and BIM. Our BIM consulting services cover the full transition — strategy, templates, and training — so your team makes the switch without losing productivity or missing project deadlines.
Teams Scaling BIM Across Offices
Your BIM workflows are inconsistent across studios or project teams. As a BIM consulting firm with multi-office experience, we develop firm-wide standards, master BEP templates, and content libraries that bring every office onto the same page.
Contractors Improving Coordination Outcomes
Your coordination process is producing too many clashes, too late. Our BIM consulting services redesign your coordination workflow, establish clash ownership protocols, and set up the federated model process that stops field conflicts before they start.
What We Deliver

Core BIM Consulting Service Areas

Every engagement is scoped to your needs. These are the core areas where Eagle BIM — a proven BIM consulting company — delivers measurable improvement on real projects.

BIM Strategy and Implementation Planning

We begin with a thorough assessment of your current capabilities, clear goal-setting, and a phased BIM implementation roadmap covering technology deployment, training schedules, and quality checkpoints. Every plan is grounded in ROI, not technical ideals.

BIM Execution Plan (BEP) Development

Our BIM consultants develop comprehensive BIM Execution Plans covering model organization, LOD progression, coordination protocols, file naming, and quality standards — aligned to AIA and BIMForum. Project-specific BEPs and firm-wide master templates are both available.

BIM Standards and Revit Template Development

We develop Revit templates with your title blocks, view templates, annotation styles, and family libraries so every project starts consistent. Standards documentation covers file organization, naming conventions, and modeling practices across your entire team.

Workflow Optimization and Process Mapping

We map your current processes, identifies bottlenecks, and redesigns coordination structures, clash resolution protocols, and deliverable workflows. Every optimized process is documented with checklists and SOPs your team can follow without guesswork.

BIM Training and Team Development

We deliver customized BIM training programs covering Revit fundamentals, coordination workflows, clash detection, and family creation. Role-specific curricula for architects, engineers, coordinators, and project managers — built around your actual projects, not generic exercises.

Technology Selection and Platform Integration

We guide technology assessments for Revit, Navisworks, BIM 360, Procore, and Revizto — evaluating features, interoperability, and total cost of ownership against your firm's actual needs. Platform setup and data exchange configuration included.
