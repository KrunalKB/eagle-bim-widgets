BIM Services Overview

One BIM Partner. Every Discipline. Every Project Stage.

Eagle BIM is a specialized BIM service provider in Texas, USA, delivering Revit BIM modeling, multi-discipline coordination, and construction documentation to architects, engineers, and contractors across the country. Our BIM services bring efficiency, technical depth, and proactive communication to every phase of the project lifecycle, from schematic design to as-built record models.

Our services are equipped with industry-standard BIM software including Autodesk Revit, Navisworks, Revizto, AutoCAD, and ReCap, the same toolset recommended as the standard by architectural, engineering, and construction firms. We believe your projects deserve more than just technical execution: they deserve a partner who brings clear communication and a proactive approach so every client receives quality, on-time delivery, consistently.

Whether you need precise architectural BIM, detailed structural modeling, MEP coordination, scan-to-BIM conversion, or end-to-end clash detection, our team builds the workflow around your project standards, BIM Execution Plan, and delivery requirements.

Multi-Discipline Coverage

Architectural, Structural & MEP BIM Services, Coordinated as One

Most BIM partners specialize in one discipline. Eagle BIM delivers all three, architectural, structural, and MEP, with the same team standards, documentation quality, and project management discipline. That means your federated model is genuinely coordinated, not stitched together from three different vendors with three different conventions.

We use Navisworks and Revizto for federated model reviews, resolving issues before they leave the model environment. Every coordination cycle produces a clear report with 3D issue snapshots that give all project stakeholders an actionable picture of what needs to be resolved, and who owns it.
