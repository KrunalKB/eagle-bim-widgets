Expert MEP BIM Services for Every Discipline and Every Project Stage

Eagle BIM delivers MEP BIM services that cover the full mechanical, electrical, plumbing, and fire protection scope — modeled in Revit MEP to your LOD requirements, project BEP standards, and fabrication tolerances. Our MEP BIM modeling produces discipline models that are clean, coordinated, and clash-ready from the moment they are delivered to your project team.

Our MEP modeling services are used by MEP engineering firms that need production modeling support, general contractors who receive 2D MEP drawings and need coordinated 3D models, and design-build teams requiring fully developed mechanical electrical plumbing BIM models for fabrication and installation.

We work as a true MEP BIM outsourcing partner — not just a modeling service. Naming conventions, system parameters, view templates, and sheet standards are all applied to your specifications before delivery. No cleanup required on arrival.

Core MEP BIM Modeling Service Areas

Every MEP BIM services engagement covers the full scope — from discipline model creation through coordination-ready delivery. Here is what is included.

Mechanical BIM Services — HVAC

Full Revit MEP mechanical models covering supply, return, and exhaust ductwork systems, AHU and FCU connections, VAV boxes, smoke control, and mechanical equipment rooms. Every duct is sized, routed, and clearance-verified — ready for clash detection and fabrication drawing extraction at LOD 300 or LOD 400.

Electrical BIM Services

Revit MEP electrical models covering low-voltage and high-voltage conduit routing, cable tray systems, distribution boards, switchgear, UPS, and lighting layouts. Circuit data, panel schedules, and load calculations derived directly from the model — all to your project standards and NEC compliance requirements.

Plumbing BIM Services

Domestic cold water, hot water, waste, vent, drainage, and sanitary systems modeled in Revit with correct slope parameters, valve placements, fixture connections, and penetration details. Pipe schedules and fixture counts extracted directly from the model for project documentation.

Fire Protection BIM Services

Complete sprinkler system MEP BIM modeling — wet and dry pipe systems, deluge, and pre-action systems. Sprinkler head layouts, main and branch pipe runs, coverage zone verification, and riser connection details — modeled to NFPA 13 requirements and coordinated with all architectural ceiling elements.

Our MEP coordination BIM service takes individual discipline models and produces a fully coordinated, clash-tested MEP package. MEP coordination BIM is built into every engagement — duct, pipe, conduit, and sprinkler all routed to fit within the architectural ceiling zone and clear of structural elements. Revit MEP Modeling

How We Build Your MEP BIM Models

Every Eagle BIM MEP BIM modeling engagement starts with a thorough review of your project information — drawings, specifications, BEP requirements, and coordination schedule. Our MEP modeling services process does not start until we understand exactly what the model needs to do and how it will be used in coordination.

Our Revit MEP modeling team works from your templates and your families wherever possible — importing your title blocks, applying your view templates, and following your naming conventions exactly. Where templates are not available, we build them to your standards before Revit MEP modeling begins.

Every MEP BIM model we produce is delivered as a complete mechanical electrical plumbing BIM package — not just individual files. That means coordinated system parameters, linked model references, clash-tested routing, and parametric schedules all included. As a mechanical electrical plumbing BIM specialist, Eagle BIM ensures every discipline model connects and performs as a unified system from day one.
