Who We Are

Full-Scope BIM Services
Powered by BIMPRO LLC

Eagle BIM is a BIM services provider operated by BIMPRO LLC, a leading BIM company headquartered in Pflugerville, Texas. We deliver precise, Revit-native BIM modeling and BIM coordination services to architectural firms, structural engineers, MEP consultants, and general contractors across the United States.

Our team specializes in the full spectrum of BIM services — from structural BIM and MEP BIM to clash detection, scan to BIM, shop drawings, and BIM consulting. Every model we deliver is built to your Level of Detail (LOD), your Revit template, and the applicable building codes for your state.

What sets Eagle BIM apart is our commitment to quality before commitment: every new BIM services engagement begins with a working sample model delivered in 48 hours — no fees, no contracts. You evaluate the work quality first. Then you decide whether to proceed.

With 1,000+ BIM projects completed across commercial, healthcare, residential, industrial, hotel, and educational sectors, Eagle BIM brings real-project BIM experience to every scope — from a single-discipline Revit model to a fully federated, multi-discipline BIM coordination package.

How Eagle BIM Services Work

From your first email to a fully coordinated, Revit-native BIM model — our process is designed to be fast, transparent, and free of surprises at every stage.

01
Share Your Drawings
Send your CAD files, PDFs, or project scope by email. We accept any format — DWG, PDF, RVT, or a plain scope description for early BIM engagement stages.
02
48-Hour BIM Sample
Within 48 hours, we return a working Revit BIM sample model built to your LOD, your template, and your project's state-specific code. No charge. No contract.
03
Review & Align
You review the BIM model quality, coordinate on scope, LOD, and deliverable format. If the work meets your standard, we proceed. If not, walk away — no obligation.
