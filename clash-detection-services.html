Clash Detection Services

Clash Detection Services That Find Every Conflict Before the Field Does

Eagle BIM delivers clash detection services that systematically test all discipline models against each other — architectural, structural, MEP, and civil — and surface every hard clash, soft clash, and workflow conflict before a single component reaches the site. Clashes that pass undetected become RFIs, change orders, and schedule delays. Our clash detection services exist to make sure they do not.

Our BIM clash detection process runs in Navisworks Manage with discipline-specific tolerance rules, clearance requirements, and priority filters applied before any report is produced. We de-duplicate results, filter out false positives, and deliver a clean, actionable clash report with every issue categorized, ownership assigned, and a resolution path defined.

Whether you need standalone clash detection on a single model set, ongoing coordination clash testing across a multi-stage project, or MEP clash detection focused on mechanical and electrical routing through a complex ceiling zone — Eagle BIM runs it accurately, on time, and with full documentation of every decision made.

Clash types we detect and resolve
Type 01 — Critical
Hard Clashes
Two elements physically occupying the same space — a duct through a structural beam, a pipe through a wall without a sleeve. Hard clashes must be resolved before construction. They cannot be worked around on site without significant cost.
Type 02 — Important
Soft Clashes
Elements that do not physically intersect but violate required clearance zones — insufficient maintenance access to equipment, MEP services too close to structural elements, or inadequate ceiling zone depth for the specified finishes.
Type 03 — Constructability
Workflow Clashes
Sequencing and constructability conflicts — elements that are geometrically clear but cannot be installed in the required construction sequence without removing previously installed components or violating trade access requirements.

Our Process

How Our Clash Detection Services Work

Every clash detection in construction project follows the same structured process. Clear stages, clear ownership, no surprises.

01
Model Intake
We receive all discipline models, verify shared coordinates and naming conventions, and set up the federated Navisworks environment. Clash rules and clearance tolerances agreed before the first test runs.
02
Clash Testing
Hard and soft clash tests run across all discipline pairs. Results are filtered, de-duplicated, and categorized by severity, discipline, and grid location. Only actionable clashes reach the report.
03
Report Delivery
Structured clash report delivered with 3D snapshots, severity ratings, and ownership assignments. Presented to the coordination team with clear resolution paths and deadlines for every open item.
04
Re-Testing
Models updated by discipline teams. We re-run clash detection to verify all resolutions and identify any new conflicts introduced by model changes. Cycle continues until all clashes are cleared.
05
Final Clearance
Final clash-free confirmation report delivered with complete resolution log, updated NWD, and full audit trail from first detection through final clearance — ready for handover or construction start.

Clash Detection in Construction

Where Clash Detection Services Find the Most Conflicts

The same areas of a building generate the majority of clashes on every project. Our clash detection in construction process focuses testing intensity where conflicts are statistically most likely — ceiling zones, structural penetrations, plant rooms, and riser shafts — so nothing slips through.

Over 300 projects of BIM clash detection experience has shown us the patterns. We know where to look, what rules to apply, and how to separate genuine conflicts from modelling artefacts that generate false clash reports — so your team spends time resolving real issues, not chasing noise.
